1 It has become our shared circumstance to frequent flat, glowing screens. As their content grows more insistent for our time and manipulative of our behavior, we begin to resemble actors directed this way and that. The film director David Fincher has been credited as saying that, “Every scene you direct … it’s the same thing: behavior over time.” Fincher’s pithy clause is as relevant for film makers as it is for digital creators — it compels us to respect the influence we have over the time and behavior of those who use our tools. It implores us to work critically and humanely. – Behaviorovetime.org
